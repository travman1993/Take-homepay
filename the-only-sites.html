Why We're Building This

The internet is filled with bloated websites, intrusive ads, and privacy-violating trackers. We believe it doesn't have to be this way. Our mission is to create a collection of simple, effective tools that respect your privacy and just work.

Our Principles

  • Privacy First: No tracking, no cookies, no data collection. All tools are static or client-side only.
  • Simple & Fast: No bloat, no frameworks, no frameworks. Just clean code that runs instantly.
  • Free & Open: All tools are free to use. No paywalls, no premium tiers.
  • Useful: We build tools for real problems, not solutions looking for problems.
