Frequently Asked Questions

Is Paycheck Clarity accurate?

Paycheck Clarity provides estimates based on commonly used tax rates and assumptions. Your actual paycheck may differ due to various factors including your specific W-4 withholdings, state/local taxes, filing status, and additional deductions. Always consult a tax professional for accurate information.

Do you store my data?

No. All calculations happen in your browser. We don't store, transmit, or share your information. Once you close the page, your data is cleared.

Is the calculator mobile-friendly?

Yes! Paycheck Clarity is fully responsive and works great on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Can I use this for my business or tax return?

Paycheck Clarity is for personal estimation only. For business accounting, tax returns, or financial planning, consult with a qualified professional (accountant, tax advisor, financial planner).

What if I find an error?

If you find a bug or calculation error, please use the contact form above to let us know. Include details about:

  • The inputs you used
  • The expected result
  • The actual result you received

Can I suggest a feature?

Absolutely! We'd love to hear your ideas. Use the contact form to share feature requests and suggestions.

Is this site free?

Yes, Paycheck Clarity is completely free to use. No signup, no hidden fees, no tricks.

Do you make money from this?

We may place advertising on the site in the future to help cover hosting costs. However, these are optional and don't affect your ability to use the calculator.
