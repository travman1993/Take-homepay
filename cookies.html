Cookie Policy for Paycheck Clarity

Last Updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your computer or device that websites use to remember information about you, track your activity, or improve user experience. There are different types of cookies, including:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary files deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or are manually deleted.
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by the website you're visiting.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services (advertisers, analytics providers, etc.).

2. Paycheck Clarity Does NOT Use Cookies

Paycheck Clarity does not use cookies. We do not set, store, or track cookies on your device. This means:

  • We don't track your visits or activity
  • We don't store your preferences or settings
  • We don't use analytics tools that rely on cookies
  • We don't use advertising cookies

3. No Tracking or Analytics

Paycheck Clarity is a static, privacy-focused application. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar tracking services
  • Advertising cookies or pixels
  • Retargeting or behavioral tracking
  • Session recording or heatmapping tools

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain optional advertising placeholders or links to third-party websites. These external services may use cookies. We are not responsible for their cookie practices. Please review their privacy policies and cookie statements for information about how they collect and use data.

5. Your Browser Settings

Even though Paycheck Clarity doesn't use cookies, most modern browsers allow you to control cookie settings:

  • Block all cookies
  • Allow cookies only from trusted sites
  • Delete cookies when closing the browser
  • View and manage stored cookies

Refer to your browser's Help or Privacy settings to adjust your cookie preferences.

6. Local Storage & Similar Technologies

Like cookies, technologies such as local storage, session storage, and similar tracking mechanisms are also not used on Paycheck Clarity. All your calculator inputs are processed in memory and cleared when you leave or refresh the page.

7. Data Retention

Paycheck Clarity does not retain any of your data. Once you close the page or refresh, all information you entered is immediately cleared from your browser's memory. No data is stored on our servers or in any persistent storage.

8. EU GDPR & Privacy Regulations

Paycheck Clarity complies with privacy regulations including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and similar laws. Since we do not collect personal data, these regulations do not impose significant requirements on our operations. However, we respect your privacy rights and are transparent about our practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our Contact page.
