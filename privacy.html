Privacy Policy for Paycheck Clarity

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Paycheck Clarity ("we," "us," "our," or "the Site") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Paycheck Clarity is a static, client-side application. All calculations are performed in your browser using JavaScript. We do not:

  • Store your paycheck data
  • Transmit your inputs to any server
  • Use cookies to track you
  • Collect your IP address
  • Use Google Analytics or similar tracking tools

3. Data Processing

Any data you enter into the calculator (salary, hourly rate, hours worked, deductions) is processed entirely within your browser's memory. Once you close the page or refresh, this data is immediately cleared. No data is saved, transmitted, or stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain optional ad placeholders or links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

5. Cookies

Paycheck Clarity does not use cookies. For more information, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. However, if you have concerns about our practices, please contact us via our Contact page.

7. Security

While we maintain reasonable security practices, please remember that no online service is 100% secure. Use Paycheck Clarity at your own risk. Do not share sensitive financial information (bank account numbers, Social Security numbers, etc.) with anyone online.

8. Children's Privacy

Paycheck Clarity is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Site constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.

Important Disclaimers

Paycheck Clarity is for informational and educational purposes only. The information provided is not financial advice, tax advice, or legal advice. We do not guarantee the accuracy of estimates. Your actual take-home pay may differ based on various factors including tax withholdings, deductions, state and local taxes, filing status, and more. Always consult with a qualified tax professional for accurate information regarding your specific situation.
